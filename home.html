    About us

    Mangasy is a mobile game that brings the exciting world of manga to life. Our game allows you to create your own team of manga characters, equip them with boosters and equipment, and challenge other players. Immerse yourself in a captivating universe where you can develop your own world, keep fit, and evolve your characters. Complete quests every day to grow your space and unlock new adventures.
